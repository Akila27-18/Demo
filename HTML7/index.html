Recipe #1 Fried Rice

Fried Rice is an Asian dish made by stir frying cooked rice in a wok with vegetables, soya sauce and sometimes with meat & eggs as well. You will find numerous versions across Asia, made with varying ingredients like onions, scallions, preserved meat, garlic etc.

  • Time: 15min
  • Serves: 2

Be ready with these Ingredients:
Rice Oil/ghee Fresh boiled Vegetables Sauces Ginger,Garlic Paste paneer/Mushroom

How to prepare:

Step 1:Soak 1 cup rice for 10 to 20 mins. If you are a large family you can make make more rice (upto 3 cups) following the same method. Add rice to a large bowl. This is the kind of basmati rice I use. You can also use any long grain or medium grain like Jasmine rice or sona masuri.

Step 2:Heat oil in wok on high heat.

Step 3:Add in ginger, garlic, spring onions, green chillies and toss well.

Step 4:Add in veggies, and cook for 5 mins on high heat till tender. Add in salt, sugar,pepper,soy sauce and vinegar and mix well.

Step 5:Add in cooked rice and toss well.

Step 6:Now add some extra pepper powder, spring onion greens and mix well.

Step 7:Serve hot.
